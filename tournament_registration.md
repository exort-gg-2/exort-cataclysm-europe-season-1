# Tournament Registration & Participation Process

## Registration

-   Teams must first complete the open registration.
-   After registration closes, teams will be ranked based on their VRS
    rating.
-   Invitations to participate will be sent starting with the teams
    holding the highest VRS rating.

## Invitation & Slot Confirmation

-   Selected teams will have **72 hours** to confirm their participation
    and secure their slot.

## Payment Process

-   A manager from **Exort** will contact selected teams.
-   Teams will be instructed to send a Direct Message to the official
    Twitter account:
    👉 https://x.com/esports_exort
-   Payment details for the participation slot will be provided **only
    through this official account**.
-   Teams must complete the payment within **72 hours** after receiving
    instructions.

## Replacement Policy

-   If a team fails to complete the payment within the given timeframe,
    the invitation will be passed to the next team in the VRS ranking.
-   If no teams with a VRS rating remain, invitations will be sent to
    teams without a VRS rating in the order of registration.
-   The same **72-hour payment window** applies.

## Security Notice

> ⚠️ For security reasons, teams must follow this process strictly to
> avoid potential fraud.

-   All official communication regarding payment will be conducted
    **exclusively** through the verified Exort Twitter account.
